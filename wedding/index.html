Our Story

Classmates and friends,
an early connection,
a lasting transformation, ever profound.

But young love is foolish,
we drifted apart,
separated,
though never far.

Through the years,
paths intertwined,
ever an instant away.
Locked in orbit,
always circling.

Always special.

A decade on,
true yearnings revealed,
fatefully reunited.
Living for each other,
journeying together,
an immense shared history.

Finally,
we affirm our love,
both original and new.

Finally,
like young sweethearts again,
just as we always were.
