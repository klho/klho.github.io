Research Highlights

  • O(N log N) “butterfly” factorization for high-frequency scattering and Fourier integral operators
  • Mathematical theory for FasL trimerism in apoptosis as the lowest order oligomer enabling irreversible cell death
  • Algebraic methods for model selection of chemical reaction networks based on data “structure”
  • Automated all-atom protein crystal structure refinement via energy minimization, hydrogen-bond network optimization, X-ray diffraction fitting, etc.
